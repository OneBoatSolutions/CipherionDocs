# Account Setup

Create your Cipherion account and configure your organization settings to get started with enterprise-grade encryption.

<Callout title="Before you begin">
Ensure you have admin privileges for your organization and access to your company email domain.
</Callout>

## Setup Process

<Steps>
<Step>
### Create Your Account

Sign up for a new Cipherion account

1. Visit the Cipherion Sign Up page
2. Enter your business email address
3. Create a strong password (minimum 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols)
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation code
</Step>

<Step>
### Organization Configuration

Set up your organization details and preferences

**Required Information:**
- Organization name and legal entity details
- Industry and compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
- Data residency preferences
- Expected data volume and usage patterns

</div>
</Step>

<Step>
### Verification & Activation

Complete account verification and activate your organization

**Final Verification Steps:**
- ✓ Email verification completed
- ✓ Organization details reviewed and approved
- ✓ Security configuration validated
- ✓ Terms of service and privacy policy accepted
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Account Setup Complete!">
Your Cipherion account is now ready. You can proceed to create your first project.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions

### Email verification not received?
Check your spam folder and ensure emails from Cipherion are whitelisted.

### Organization not detected automatically?
Contact our support team with your domain details for manual verification.

### 2FA setup issues? (Coming Soon...)
Ensure your device time is synchronized and try using a different authenticator app.
